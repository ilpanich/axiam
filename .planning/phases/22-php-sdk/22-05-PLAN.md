| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-22-17 | Tampering / Information Disclosure | gRPC channel TLS | high | mitigate | Strict-TLS channel credentials only; no insecure channel construction (§6/D-12) |
