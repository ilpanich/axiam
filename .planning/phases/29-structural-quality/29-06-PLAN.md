<objective>

reconstruction with rcgen's real-PEM parse (from_ca_cert_pem), removing the latent issuer-DN
drift risk (D-08), and consolidate the byte-identical keypair/fingerprint/AES-256-GCM helpers

(D-03) — proven signing-equivalent by an identical-issuer-DN test. This plan also carries the
phase-end full-workspace regression gate (D-06).

Purpose: Remove a Tampering/Repudiation drift vector in cert issuance (ASVS V6) and eliminate
crypto-helper triplication without altering the AES-256-GCM scheme.

Output: crates/axiam-pki/src/crypto.rs; rcgen x509-parser feature enabled; from_ca_cert_pem
reconstruction; the identical-issuer-DN test; and a green `cargo test --workspace` regression gate.
